Common Questions

How do I start using Cervi Tracker?

Open Cervi Tracker on the App Store for iPhone and iPad, or use the web app on other devices. Then create an account with your email and complete the brief onboarding to set up your profile.

Is my medical data private?

Yes. Your data is stored securely and is never shared with third parties. PDF reports are generated locally on your device. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I generate a physician report?

Navigate to the Hub tab in the app. You'll find three report options: General Summary, Blood Tests Report, and Tumor Dynamics. Tap the one you want to preview and print it directly from the app.

Can Cervi Tracker give me medical advice?

No. Cervi Tracker is a personal monitoring and tracking tool only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnoses, or treatment recommendations. All clinical decisions must be made with a qualified healthcare professional. See our Terms of Use for more information.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all associated data from within the app under Settings → Delete Account. This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
